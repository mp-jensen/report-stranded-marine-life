Report Stranded Marine Life Application Plan

Team

The team that created this plan consists of five students from Oregon State University in CS 361 Software Engineering I. These students developed a plan using the waterfall software development framework based on an application proposal by the customer (another student in the program), Justin Tromp. The team members are:

  • Brittany Abad
  • Lauren Boone
  • Christoper Feth
  • Manda Jensen
  • Kunal Patadia

Documents

In the links below you can download the documents that were created as part of the software development life cycle of the product during the class. The documents go from oldest document to most recent document. All documents are a compilation of work from the five team members aside from the application proposal, that document is the sole work of the customer. Customer feedback was provided throughout the design process to ensure the final result reflects the goals of the customer.
